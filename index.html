      Safe sale tips

      Quick checklist before you message or buy.

      • Meet in public (police “safe exchange” zones are best), bring a friend, avoid sharing your home address for high-value items.
      • Use safe payments: cash in person (count it), or protected methods. Avoid wire transfers, gift cards, “courier will pay”, and overpayment/refund scams.
      • Verify the item before paying: test it, check serial/IMEI where applicable, and confirm it’s not locked (e.g., iCloud/Find My).
      • Keep it in-app: don’t move to WhatsApp/email early, and keep the agreement in chat.
      • Watch red flags: urgency, refusing to meet, “someone else will pick it up”, or unusual payment instructions.
